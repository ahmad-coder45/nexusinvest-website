General Questions

What is NexusInvest?
NexusInvest is a cryptocurrency mining investment platform that allows individuals to invest in crypto mining operations and earn daily profits without the technical complexities of setting up and maintaining mining equipment.
How does NexusInvest work?
You create an account, deposit funds, choose an investment plan, and start earning daily profits. We handle all the mining operations using professional-grade equipment, and you receive your share of the profits automatically every day.
Is NexusInvest safe and legitimate?
Yes, NexusInvest is a legitimate platform with real mining operations. We use bank-level security, encrypted transactions, and transparent operations. All investments are backed by actual mining hardware and operations.

Investment Questions

What is the minimum investment amount?
The minimum investment is $5 with our Plan 01 - Basic Miner. We offer 7 different plans ranging from $5 to $550 to suit different investment capacities.
What is the ROI and investment duration?
All our plans offer 80% ROI over 30 days. You receive daily profits automatically, and after 30 days, your investment is complete. You can then reinvest or withdraw your earnings.
Can I invest in multiple plans?
Yes! You can invest in multiple plans simultaneously. There's no limit to how many investments you can have active at the same time.

Deposits & Withdrawals

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept three payment methods: Binance (USDT), JazzCash, and EasyPaisa. You can choose the most convenient method for you.
How long does deposit approval take?
Deposits are usually approved within 24 hours after you upload payment proof. Our admin team reviews each deposit to ensure security and accuracy.
What are the withdrawal restrictions?
You can make maximum 2 withdrawals per month. First withdrawal is available 10 days after your first investment. Second withdrawal requires at least 1 active referral. A 5% tax is applied to all withdrawals.
What is the minimum withdrawal amount?
The minimum withdrawal amount is $5. You can withdraw your earnings anytime as long as you meet the withdrawal requirements.

Referrals & Salary

How does the referral program work?
Our 3-level referral program pays: Level 1 (direct referrals) - 12%, Level 2 - 2%, Level 3 - 1%. You earn commissions whenever your referrals make investments.
What are the salary plans?
Salary plans are based on your direct sales: Plan 1 ($50-$99) = $5/week, Plan 2 ($100-$249) = $10/week, Plan 3 ($250-$499) = $20/week, Plan 4 ($500+) = $40/week. Salaries are paid every Sunday.
Do I get a bonus for joining?
Yes! Every new user receives a $1 welcome bonus upon registration. This bonus is automatically added to your account balance.
